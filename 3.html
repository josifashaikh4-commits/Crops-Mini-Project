How It Works

AI Smart Crop Disease Detection Working Process

🌱 Working Process

Step 1 – Upload Image

The farmer uploads a clear image of the crop leaf.

Step 2 – Image Processing

The system resizes, cleans, and enhances the uploaded image.

Step 3 – AI Analysis

A trained CNN model analyzes the image and extracts important features.

Step 4 – Disease Prediction

The AI predicts whether the leaf is healthy or identifies the disease.

Step 5 – Recommendation

  • Disease Name
  • Confidence Score
  • Causes
  • Prevention Tips
  • Recommended Treatment
