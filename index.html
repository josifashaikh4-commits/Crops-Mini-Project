AI Smart Crop Disease Detection

Smart AI technology that helps farmers detect crop diseases quickly, accurately, and efficiently for better farming.

Welcome

AI Smart Crop Disease Detection is an intelligent web application that helps farmers identify crop diseases quickly using Artificial Intelligence (AI). Farmers simply upload an image of a plant leaf, and the system analyzes it to detect diseases and provide treatment suggestions.

This technology reduces crop loss, improves productivity, and supports sustainable farming by enabling early disease detection.

Key Features

🌱 Upload Images

Upload crop leaf images for AI analysis.

🤖 AI Detection

Detect plant diseases using Machine Learning.

⚡ Instant Results

Receive disease prediction within seconds.

💊 Treatment

Get treatment and prevention recommendations.

📊 Dashboard

Simple and easy-to-use interface.

📱 Mobile Friendly

Accessible from smartphones, tablets and PCs.

Our Mission

To empower farmers with smart AI technology that ensures healthy crops, increases agricultural productivity, and improves food security through accurate disease detection and timely treatment recommendations.
