About AI Smart Crop Disease Detection

Artificial Intelligence for Smart Agriculture

📖 Project Overview

AI Smart Crop Disease Detection uses Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques to identify diseases in crop leaves from uploaded images. The AI model is trained using thousands of images of healthy and diseased plants.


The system compares the uploaded image with its trained knowledge and predicts the disease with high accuracy, helping farmers take quick action to protect their crops.

🎯 Objectives

🌱 Detect crop diseases at an early stage
🌾 Reduce crop damage
⏰ Save farmers' time and money
🥬 Improve crop quality
🤖 Support smart agriculture using AI
